Voting Day Experience

Step by Step Booth Guide

1

🧾 Check your name & booth

Confirm name on Electoral Roll at voters.eci.gov.in or call 1950. Use Voter Helpline App Booth Navigator for GPS directions.

2

🧍 Stand in queue orderly

Separate queues exist for senior citizens and differently-abled voters.

3

✅ Identity verification

Polling Officer checks your name in voter list + verifies ID.

4

☝️ Ink mark + ticket + signature

Indelible ink on LEFT hand finger, receive ticket, give signature.

5

🚪 Enter voting compartment

Hand over ticket, Presiding Officer activates Ballot Unit, you enter alone — completely private.

6

🔵 Press the BLUE button

Press the blue button next to your chosen candidate's name and symbol.

7

🔴 RED light + BEEP

A red light glows and a beep sounds confirming your vote. If NO light or beep → immediately inform the Presiding Officer.

8

🧾 Verify on VVPAT

Slip shows candidate name, symbol and number for exactly 7 seconds. Slip is NOT given to you — it auto-drops into a sealed box.

Note: "NOTA (None Of The Above) is always the LAST option on the EVM."

Will anyone know how I voted?

EVM records only a number — not your name, face, or ID. Protected by the Representation of the People Act, 1951. Even the President of India's vote cannot be traced.

Your vote is 100% anonymous — always.

Feeling threatened? Call Election Commission Helpline: 1950

🪪 What is your EPIC Number?

EPIC = Elector's Photo Identity Card — unique ID on your Voter ID card.

  • Find your Assembly Constituency and Polling Station
  • Search your name in Electoral Roll
  • Know your Polling Officials (DEO, ERO, BLO)
  • Apply for Voter ID correction
  • Apply for shifting within constituency
